Gameathlon 2025

August 30, 2025

We’re proud to announce that Team Cryst has been officially invited to GameAthlon 2025 in Athens, Greece! The event will take place at the legendary Tae Kwon Do Stadium between the 12-14th of September, bringing together some of the best teams, players, and gaming fans from all over Europe. This is a huge milestone for us, and we can’t wait to represent our team and community on such a massive stage. Stay tuned for updates as we prepare to bring our best game to gameathlon!
